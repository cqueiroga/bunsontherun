Welcome to Buns on the Run!

We wanted to explore recipes we’d never tried before while recording our mistakes and misfortunes, all with a sense of humor and the ability to laugh at ourselves. We started our YouTube channel to be able to show you our goofiness in action and not long after that we decided to branch out and include makeup reviews and challenges, book recommendations and even a little bit of fandom related humor. We are two young amateur cooks who like to accidentally set things on fire and dance in the smoke. Join us on our journey as we create and destroy recipes we find on the internet.
