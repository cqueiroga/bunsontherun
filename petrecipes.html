Frozen Yogurt Treats

Original Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup and 2 ounces vanilla or plain yogurt
(I used vanilla Chobani Greek Yogurt)
¼ cup peanut butter
½ Tablespoon honey
6 baby carrots (peeled and finely chopped)
*You can also add in a mashed banana but I didn’t have any on hand.

Bake Time:
Place on a baking sheet and freeze, at least 1 hour.

1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9
  1. Start by chopping the carrots. I find that it's a lot easier to peel the baby carrots first and then chop the thin slices very finely.
  2. Next scoop the yogurt into a mixing bowl.
  3. Add peanut butter,
  4. honey,
  5. and chopped carrots.
  6. Stir until they are all combined.
  7. Now, line a mini baking tray with mini cupcake liners or use a mini silicone mold if you have one.
  8. Scoop the yogurt mixture evenly into each liner and smooth the tops down with a spoon.
  9. Keep checking the tray and when they no longer feel sticky you can remove them. Keep them stored in a tray or plastic bag in the freezer for those hot summer days, late night cravings or simply whenever you want to reward your pooch with a frozen treat!
